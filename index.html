About Us

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the team behind Pragathi Micro Finance.

Pragathi Micro Finance is committed to financial inclusion for underserved communities in Sri Lanka. Our mission is to empower individuals and small businesses through accessible financial solutions that promote economic growth and stability.

With a team of financial experts dedicated to customer success, we provide transparent, reliable, and user-friendly microfinance services tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.
